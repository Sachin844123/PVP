About PVP eSport

Founded in 2024, PVP eSport emerged from a shared passion for Free Fire and competitive gaming. What started as a group of friends playing together has evolved into one of the most promising eSports teams in the region.

Our core values center around teamwork, strategic gameplay, and continuous growth. Based in India, we've been actively competing in the Free Fire eSports scene for over 2 years, constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the battlegrounds.
